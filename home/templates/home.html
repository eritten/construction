our services

At Halleluya Properties Limited, we offer a comprehensive range of construction and renovation services to meet your specific needs. Our team of highly skilled professionals is dedicated to delivering top-notch solutions that exceed your expectations. Here's a detailed look at the services we provide:

Bricklaying

Our experienced bricklayers are experts in creating sturdy and aesthetically pleasing brickwork for various construction projects. We use top-quality materials and the latest techniques to ensure durability and precision.

General Civil Engineering

We offer a wide range of civil engineering services, including structural analysis, foundation design, and infrastructure development, to ensure the structural integrity of your project.

Plastering

We specialize in plastering services that transform your walls and ceilings into smooth, flawless surfaces. Our skilled plasterers use advanced methods to achieve a flawless finish for both interior and exterior spaces.

Laying Underground Heating Pipes

We specialize in the installation of underground heating pipes to provide efficient heating solutions for your property. Our advanced techniques ensure even heat distribution and energy efficiency.

Electric Wiring

Our certified electricians handle all aspects of electrical work, from wiring installations to repairs and maintenance. Safety and efficiency are our top priorities when it comes to electrical services.

Architectural Designing/AutoCAD

Our team of architects and designers utilize cutting-edge AutoCAD technology to create detailed and innovative architectural designs that bring your vision to life.

Setting Out of Utilities

We assist in the precise placement and layout of utilities to ensure optimal functionality and efficiency in your construction or renovation project.

General Site Surveying

Our expert surveyors conduct comprehensive site surveys to gather essential data for project planning and execution. Accurate surveys are the foundation of successful construction projects.
